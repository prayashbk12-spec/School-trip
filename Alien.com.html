Welcome to Jaaduu.com

Jaaduu = Ankit Thapa Xetri






Jaadu/Ankit is the beloved, blue-skinned alien from the 2003 Bollywood sci-fi film Koi... Mil Gaya, who gifts supernatural powers to the protagonist, Rohit Mehra (Hrithik Roshan). Played by 3-foot-tall actor Ankit Thapa Xetri, the character was created using an intricate animatronic suit. Jaadu is known for his signature phrase "dhoop" (sunshine).
